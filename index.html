FAQ

Common questions for guests.

What’s the difference between Golden Experience and VIP?
Golden includes early entry, specialty cocktails sampling (7–9), limited bar seating (7–9), VIP check-in after 9, cocktail-making demo, and the Roots Toast. VIP includes all of that plus extended limited bar seating (7 PM – 2 AM), upgraded cups, swag bag, and reserved seating/private section.
Do all tickets include tasting bites and a complimentary drink?
Yes — all tiers include tasting bites (while supplies last) and one complimentary drink.
Can I upgrade at the door?
If upgrades are available, staff can assist. We recommend purchasing your desired tier in advance.
